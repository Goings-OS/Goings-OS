# Keep It Goings Consulting: Credit Audit & Capital Funding SOP
# LOCATION: 01_keep_it_goings_consulting/credit_audit_sop.md
# GOVERNANCE: Private Infrastructure / Always Positive

## 1. Core Objective
Convert inbound leads into active auditing clients through automated value demonstration and the enforcement of the FCRA (Fair Credit Reporting Act) dispute parameters.

## 2. Inbound Communication Guardrails
* Never guarantee a specific credit score increase.
* Always frame the service as a "Forensic Credit Profile Audit".
* Reframe all negative items as "temporary operational variables" that can be legally challenged via 15 U.S.C. § 1681.

## 3. The 72-Hour Intake Pipeline
* **Step 1:** Lead submits native GHL intake form.
* **Step 2:** System routes lead data to Port 5000 webhook middleware.
* **Step 3:** GHL sends automated welcome text: "Hello, we received your file. Our automated systems are evaluating your profile for FCRA compliance. A secure booking link will follow shortly."
* **Step 4:** System validates capital ingress and logs the owner's draw distribution into the private ledger.
