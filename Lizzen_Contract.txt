This Talent Engagement Agreement is made, entered into, and made effective this 16th day of June, 2026, by and between Norfolk Takeover Cruise LLC (hereinafter referred to as the "PURCHASER") and Lizzen (hereinafter referred to as the "ARTIST"). This document constitutes the sole, primary, and entire binding agreement between the parties regarding the commercial services detailed herein.

1. VENUE DESIGNATION AND PRODUCTION PARAMETERS
The one hour live tracking performance and subsequent meet and greet engagement are designated to take place strictly within the main shipboard theater of the vessel, the Carnival Sunshine. PURCHASER shall provide professional theater-grade audio, engineering, staging, and lighting infrastructure. ARTIST agrees to cooperate and participate in all scheduled technical alignment sessions and sound checks as reasonably directed by the General Manager or production staff.

2. PROMOTIONAL CONTENT AND QUANTIFIED MEDIA DELIVERABLES
To drive maximum commercial velocity and eliminate any operational ambiguity, the ARTIST agrees to execute and deliver a specific, quantified suite of promotional media assets. These marketing assets shall be developed and deployed in strict coordination with the K.I.G. Media Wing and must consist of the following items:
* Two (2) custom professional audio Radio Drops configured for regional broadcast network rotation.
* Two (2) high-definition video Drops welcoming and verifying the engagement to the NTC 400 manifest.
* Three (3) high-resolution promotional Photos for Flyers with unrestricted physical and digital marketing layout licensing.
* Twelve (12) strategic Story Posts distributed across the promotional lifecycle as directed by the corporate marketing calendar.
* Three (3) permanent, non-archived Main Page Feed Posts on the ARTIST's primary official social networks.

Public broadcast and deployment of these materials shall commence immediately upon the mutual execution of this contract packet and the verification of the initial binder fee asset clearing.

3. UPGRADED ACCOMMODATION OVERHEAD
In lieu of standard or generic housing layouts, the PURCHASER shall fully secure, allocate, and provide one (1) premium Balcony Cabin aboard the vessel to comfortably house up to three (3) total artist personnel traveling within the official delegation. This travel allocation incorporates full shipboard dining privileges, baseline room amenities, and international maritime passage for the entirety of the five day voyage itinerary from Norfolk, Virginia to Bermuda.

4. GOVERNING LAW, PRIVATE COMPLIANCE, AND JURISDICTION
This Agreement and all subsequent operational execution nodes shall be governed, interpreted, and enforced strictly in accordance with the laws of the Commonwealth of Virginia. Any formal claim, dispute, or legal proceeding arising out of or relating to this engagement shall be filed and resolved exclusively within the appropriate courts or arbitration bodies located closest to the PURCHASER's primary corporate base at 4010B Victory Blvd, Portsmouth, VA 23701. Both parties hereby waive any right to alternate geographic or state jurisdictions.

5. CORE FINANCIAL PARAMETERS AND BINDER ALLOCATION
The total gross investment allocation fee for the completed execution of all performance and media deliverables is set at 12,000.00 USD. To immediately secure and retain the requested market, date, and contractual price point, an initial binder fee payment of 1,000.00 USD is required from the PURCHASER. This binder fee functions as an immediate credit toward the total allocation, with all remaining balance schedules subject to ongoing private operational payment structures as finalized by the enterprise entities.
